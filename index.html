About Juliette

Technologically-inclined female who loves both web-related things and culinary arts. Programming and recipes are my thing! Currently learning about web development.

What I Do

Food Service is a passion of mine! I am a homemaker, so of course I manage the household. I solve problems, I cook, I clean. Most people in Food Service aren't very strong with computers, but I am. I also have restaurant experience in dough production and management. I love cooking and cannot go a day without doing it.

Programming is tricky, yet addictive for me. I have worked with Python and C programming (but also have lightly worked with SQL) and just recently improved my CSS skills. JavaScript is the current language I am learning. I really have come to enjoy learning and applying SEO as well. My favorite aspect of programming is how it makes any job easier!

What I Have Done

I am subscribed to a few web development newsletters and I use these resources to not only stay up-do-date with current trends, but also, as I learn new things, I blog for Let's Web Develop Together with Moritz Zimmer. I am actively involved with two cooking websites, Juliette's Recipes and Copy & Pasta. Both of these websites are a collaboration between myself and Moritz Zimmer. I had a lot to do with the content creation for these websites. I also have one other web development (WordPress) website that is full of lots of helpful links that relate to web development: Web Dev Cat's Blog. While it is a simple website, it very much demonstrates what a thorough researcher I am when it comes to learning and being able to make use of what I find.
