Juliette's Recipes

Ever want to make something and you find yourself flipping through a recipe book, wondering what would be a good choice? This website is designed to help you make practical cooking decisions. Minimizing waste and fresh product are constant concerns with perishable items. With an ingredient search, you can create menus and balanced nutritious meals, based on what is on hand.

Juliette's Recipes Blog: A Companion Website

This is a companion site to Juliette's Recipes. Here you can find some of the behind-the-scenes details of what I am doing to bring you Juliette's Recipes. Supplemental information on how to cook, how to plan both nutritious and tasty meals, and anything else interesting related to food is discussed here.

Let's Web Develop Together!

Greetings from Moritz and Juliette! The web is constantly changing, and we are always learning together! About Me

Hi, I'm Juliette. I love both web-related things and culinary arts. Programming and recipes are my thing! Currently learning about web development.

What I Do

Food Service is a passion of mine! I am a homemaker, so of course I manage the household. I solve problems, I cook, I clean. Most people in Food Service aren't very strong with computers, but I am. I also have restaurant experience in dough production and management. I love cooking and cannot go a day without doing it. Programming is tricky, yet addictive for me. I have worked with Python and C programming (but also have lightly worked with SQL) and just recently improved my CSS skills. JavaScript is the current language I am learning. I really have come to enjoy working with and applying SEO as well. My favorite aspect of programming is how it makes any job easier!

What I Have Done

I am subscribed to a few web development newsletters and try to follow the latest restaurant news. I use these resources to not only stay up-do-date with current trends, but also, as I learn new things, I blog about it. I am actively involved with cooking in general and have a cooking website: Juliette's Recipes. While I am largely responsible for the content there, it is a project that both myself and Moritz Zimmer collaborated upon. I also have one other web development (WordPress) website that is full of lots of helpful links that relate to web development: Web Dev Cat's Blog. While it is a simple website, it very much demonstrates what a thorough researcher I am when it comes to learning and being able to make use of what I find.
